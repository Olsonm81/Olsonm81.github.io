National Parks across America

Grand Canyon

The Grand Canyon National Park is located in Arizona. It is home to many different species of plants and animals. This National Park was formed by long term erosion. This Canyon is estimated to be 250 million years old.

Top 3 Animals found here

  1. Bighorn Sheep
  2. California Condor
  3. Tassel-Eared Squirrel

Yellowstone National Park

Yellowstone was America's first National Park. It's mostly located in Wyoming, but parts of the park also spread into Montana and Idaho. One of the parks most well known attraction is Old Faithful which is one of the geysers in the park. Along with the geysers Yellowstone is also known for its local wildlife.

Top 3 Animals found here

  1. Bison
  2. Grizzly Bear
  3. Gray Wolves
